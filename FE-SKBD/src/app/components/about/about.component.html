Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

You can easily track your order by following these steps:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to 'My Orders' section
  3. Click on the specific order you want to track
  4. Click the 'Track Order' button

You'll see real-time updates about your order's status, including:

  • Order confirmation
  • Payment verification
  • Processing
  • Shipping
  • Delivery

You'll also receive email notifications for each stage of the delivery process.

What is your return and exchange policy?

Return Period:

  • 30 days for unused items in original packaging
  • 7 days for defective items

Conditions for Returns:

  • Item must be unused and in original condition
  • Original tags and packaging must be intact
  • Proof of purchase required

Exchange Process:

  1. Initiate return through your account
  2. Print return shipping label
  3. Ship item back
  4. Choose replacement item or refund

Refund Timeline:

  • 3-5 business days after we receive the return
  • Original payment method will be refunded

Note: Shipping costs for returns are customer's responsibility unless item is defective.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to most countries worldwide.

International Shipping Policy:

  • We ship to most countries worldwide
  • Shipping costs vary depending on the destination and the weight of the items
  • We use reliable shipping partners

Note: International shipping may have additional customs and import duties that are the responsibility of the customer.
